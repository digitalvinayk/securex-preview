Overview

Protect What Matters, 24 Hours a Day

At Securex, we deliver high quality CCTV solutions designed to protect your property, people and assets. Whether you are securing a home, business or construction site, our tailored camera systems give you expert installation and ongoing support.

We work exclusively with proven brands like Hikvision and design each system around your site layout, risk profile and budget. Every camera we fit is positioned for maximum coverage, tested on the day of installation, and backed by our maintenance service.

All installations meet data protection regulations and can be linked to your mobile phone for real time viewing from anywhere in the world.

Key Features

What You Get With Every Install

Six features included as standard across all our CCTV systems.

HD & 4K Resolution

Crystal clear footage with professional grade cameras. Capture faces, number plates and fine detail.

Remote Viewing

Watch live and recorded footage from your smartphone, tablet or PC, anywhere in the world.

Night Vision

Infra red and low light cameras that deliver clear footage even in total darkness.

Motion Detection

Intelligent motion alerts sent to your phone the moment activity is detected on your property.

Cloud or Local Storage

Your choice of secure cloud storage or on site recording with encrypted drives.

Two Way Audio

Selected cameras let you talk to visitors or deter intruders directly from your phone.
